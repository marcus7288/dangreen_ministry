Called to Serve Small Towns

I am Dan Green, and I've dedicated my life to serving rural America through Rural Compassion. We've been working alongside local churches and community leaders to bring hope and practical help to small towns that often feel forgotten.

My approach focuses on empowering local churches to become serving centers in their communities. I believe that when churches move outside their four walls and follow Christ's example of service, entire communities are transformed.

Through training, resourcing, and partnership, I help rural pastors and church leaders dream big dreams for their communities and develop sustainable ministries that address real needs while sharing the Gospel.

Our Ministry Focus

Empowering rural churches to strengthen their communities through compassionate service

Church Serve Training

Equipping rural churches to move outside their walls and serve their communities with practical ministry solutions and strategic partnerships.

School Partnerships

Helping churches become the best friend of their local public schools through volunteer programs, resource support, and community engagement.

Community Stakeholder Relations

Building bridges between churches and community leaders, business owners, law enforcement, and other local influencers for collaborative impact.

Compassion Ministries

Providing resources and training for food pantries, clothing distribution, disaster relief, and other practical ways to meet community needs.

Leadership Development

Mentoring rural pastors and church leaders through coaching, conferences, and ongoing support to maximize their community impact.

Vision Casting

Helping small town churches discover their unique potential and develop customized strategies to transform their communities.
